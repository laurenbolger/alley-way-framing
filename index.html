  • Directions

    We are located at 1205 Washington Ave in historic downtown Golden. The entrance to our shop is tucked in the alley behind the Clothes Mine Shope. To reach us, Head north down main street (Washington Ave) turn right on 12th st. (next to Golden Goods) and walk for half a block. You will see our signs on the wall of the buildings at the mouth of the alley.

  • Parking

    We have one small parking spot in the alley next to the store for quick pick-ups or drop-offs only. For longer parking, there is a parking garage and a small outside lot located just half a block away from us on the corner of 12th and Jackson St. Street parking is also available along Washington Ave.

Services

We strive to offer a plethora of framing options to suit all of your framing needs. Our services include:

  • Framing photos, prints, posters, watercolor/acrylic/oil paintings, needle point and small quilt designs
  • Shadow boxes
  • Stretching oil, acrylic, and photo canvases
  • Floating pictures
  • Glass framing/mounting and glass replacement single, double, and triple matting and framing
  • Mounting pictures (dry or wet technique) and museum mounting
  • Mirror framing
